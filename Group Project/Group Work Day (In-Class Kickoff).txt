Group Work Day (In-Class Kickoff) 
  
On March 26 (2:30–3:45 PM), the entire class period will be dedicated to your first official group meeting.

During this time, your group will receive access to your two assigned academic papers and begin building the foundation for your project.

This is a graded working session. Your goal is to leave class with a clear, structured plan.

Objective
By the end of class, your group should demonstrate that you have:

Engaged with the assigned papers

Begun interpreting the topic

Established a clear direction for your project

Required Deliverable – 20 Points
One group submission (1–2 pages or structured bullet format) that includes:

Initial Understanding of Topic
A clear, plain-English explanation of your assigned AI risk

Key ideas or insights drawn from the academic papers

Key Themes & Direction
3–5 major themes, questions, or angles your group will explore

These should go beyond definitions (focus on meaning, implications, or tensions)

Team Plan & Roles
Names of all group members

Clear division of responsibilities

Expectations
This should reflect real progress made during class time

Your work should be thoughtful, not rushed or generic

AI can assist, but your submission should reflect your group’s understanding and direction
